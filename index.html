About CUSB

Central university of south bihar is located in Gaya Bihar India.


Vision:-

To develop enlightened citizenship of a knowledge society for peace and prosperity of individuals, nation and the world, through promotion of innovation, creative endeavors, and scholarly inquiry and to be global destination for higher education and research.


Agriculture and development department

Vision:-

In the heart of the agrarian landscape, Bihar stands as one of the most agriculturally driven states in our country. However, in the southern region of Bihar, a void exists in the form of a lack of government institutions catering to the evolving educational needs of the people. Recognizing this gap, our institution is not just focused on nurturing brilliant minds but also on disseminating knowledge throughout society. Our mission goes beyond conventional boundaries; we aspire to bring forth a new era of learning that extends beyond textbooks and traditional education. With a commitment to spreading knowledge that is innovative and forward-thinking, we aim to empower individuals and contribute to the holistic development of the community. Join us in this journey of exploration, discovery, and a shared pursuit of knowledge that transcends the ordinary.


Faculty

About

Agriculture and development department in CUSB is started in 2023. it is currently for UG course- B.Sc.Agriculture(hons.) a four year program, there are 60 seats in this program. admits students through its own counseling process, primarily based on CUET (Central University Entrance Test) scores. This approach ensures a systematic and standardized evaluation of students' academic abilities, contributing to a fair and transparent admission process..
